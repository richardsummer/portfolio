A Little About Me

Incase you missed the biggest words on the page, my name is Richard. I was born and raised in upstate South Carolina where my wife Christin and I just finished building our first house.

Even though I am a recent graduate of Carolina Code School, I see myself as a lifelong student. I enjoy solving problems, and I have a burning passion for learning new things.

In my spare time I enjoy playing with our crazy yorkie pups. When they are tuckered out you can usually find me with a laptop in hand, researching anything from programming to world history.

I enjoy working with others and I believe I would be a valuable addition to any team with the patience to show me their way. I love meeting new people and I would love to speak with you!
